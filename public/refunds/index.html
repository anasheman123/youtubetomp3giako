Refund Policy

Support payments or donations are voluntary and are intended to help keep GtubeVersor online and improving.

If a donation is duplicated, processed in error, or a manually assigned supporter tier is never delivered after we confirm payment, contact support and we will review the case.

Refund requests may be denied where there is evidence of abuse, fraud, repeated payment disputes, or when the supporter benefit has already been manually delivered.

If an external payment provider is used, refunds may also be subject to that provider's dispute and refund rules.
